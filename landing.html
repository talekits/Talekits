How it works

Three steps to a story ritual

01
🧒

Build your child's profile

Tell us about your child — their name, age, interests, and appearance — then browse the Imagination Library to pick their favourite themes, story types, and worlds to explore.

02
✨

Kit writes their story

Every day Kit the fox crafts a brand-new adventure — complete with illustrations and optional audio narration — drawn from your child's profile and chosen themes.

03
📬

It lands in your inbox

A beautifully designed PDF storybook and narrated audio file arrive each day, ready to read together whenever the moment is right. Every story is saved forever in your archive.
